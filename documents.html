Game Flow One-Pager

As a study exercise, I had to describe the game flow of a game on one page. I chose to summarize "Brothers: A Tale of two Sons" on that page. I highlighted the locations, NPC, enemies and gameplay mechanics of each level. The real challenge was to describe the controls without a word.

Balancing One-Pager

As a study exercise, I designed units of a new faction for a new fictive Warcraft game. I chose the Murlocs as a new faction and added a new elemental feature as a unique ability for this faction. This exercise aimed to describe 10 units in detail on one page.
