SUMMARY

Play as a jellyfish in an underwater world filled with anthropomorphic fish.

You've been hired by Boss, a finance shark, to convince Mairead, the owner of an independent bar, to leave the establishment and sell it to Boss !
Look for info by talking to different characters and by clicking on interactive elements. But beware, your time is limited, so don't linger too much on insignificant details or the bar will get destroyed !
